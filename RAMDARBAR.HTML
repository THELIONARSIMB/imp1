About Ram Darbar

Our Mission

Ram Darbar is dedicated to serving the community through devotion to Lord Ram and compassionate service to those in need. Founded and led by Asitya Singh, our organization operates in Laheriasarai, Darbhanga, Bihar, with the noble mission of combining spiritual devotion with social welfare.

Our Vision

We envision a community where no one goes hungry and everyone has access to spiritual guidance. Through our Ram Mandir and food distribution programs, we strive to create a society built on the principles of compassion, service, and devotion.

Founder's Message

Asitya Singh

Founder & Director

"Service to humanity is service to God. Every meal we serve, every prayer we offer, brings us closer to the ideals taught by Lord Ram."
